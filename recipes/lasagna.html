Lasagana Recipe

A delicious lasagna recipe from your grandmother

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Noodles
  • Boiling water

Steps

  1. Boil water. Put noodles in for 8-10 minutes.
  2. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
  3. Spread meat on bottom of baking dish. Place noodles on top. Put more meat on top of noodles.
  4. Bake in preheated oven for 25 minutes.
